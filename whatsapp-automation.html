What We Offer

End-to-End WhatsApp Solutions

Lead Capture & Qualification

Train our AI agents on your documents and FAQs. Instantly answer customer queries 24/7 in multiple languages, including Hindi and English, reducing human workload by up to 80%.

Showcase your entire product catalog directly within WhatsApp. Allow customers to browse, select items, and complete orders without ever leaving the chat interface.

Send personalized drip campaigns and broadcast messages to segments of your audience. Automatically re-engage cold leads or send abandoned cart reminders.

The Workflow

How The Magic Happens

1

User Initiates Chat

A user clicks on your Facebook/Instagram ad or a link on your website, instantly opening a WhatsApp chat with a predefined message.

2

AI Agent Engages

The ArsuAI AI agent responds within 2 seconds. It greets the user, analyzes their intent, and begins a personalized conversational flow.

3

Data Collection & CRM Sync

The agent collects crucial details (name, requirements, budget). This data is automatically pushed to your CRM (e.g., HubSpot, Zoho) or Google Sheets in real-time.

4

Conversion / Handover

If the user is ready, the AI helps them complete a purchase or book a meeting. If the query is complex, it seamlessly hands the chat over to a human agent with full context.
